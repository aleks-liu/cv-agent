# Revolut - Company Research Summary

## Company Overview
Revolut is a global fintech company founded in 2015 by Nik Storonsky and Vlad Yatsenko. The company provides digital banking services and has grown to serve 65+ million personal customers and 500k+ business customers across 100+ countries.

## Recent Milestones
- **2024**: Reached 50+ million customers, most downloaded finance app in 19 countries, processed $1+ trillion in customer transactions
- **2023**: Launched in Brazil & New Zealand, launched Ultra tier plan in EEA

## Company Scale
- 10,000+ employees worldwide
- 100+ different nationalities
- 30+ countries hiring
- 25+ hubs around the world
- 8 locations certified as Great Place To Work (2023)

## Core Culture Values (5 Principles)

### 1. Never Settle
- Set high goals, strive for excellence
- Develop unique ideas, move beyond "the traditional way"
- Can-do attitude, view setbacks as opportunities to learn

### 2. Dream Team
- Collaborative, team-first mentality
- Never compromise on talent
- Honest, direct, and respectful communication
- Diverse perspectives celebrated

### 3. Think Deeper
- Logic and reason prevail
- Dive into details, gather data, iterate
- Be logical AND innovative
- Think long-term, be open-minded

### 4. Get It Done
- Ideas are great, but execution is everything
- Respect comes from "sweat and stretch"
- Achieve goals fast, end-to-end
- Act like an owner

### 5. Deliver WOW
- Customers at the heart of everything
- Prioritize quality above quantity
- Meticulous attention to detail
- Customer-centric solutions

## Tech Stack (from Java Engineer context)
- **Language**: Java 21
- **Database**: PostgreSQL (via jOOQ), Redis
- **Infrastructure**: Kubernetes, Docker, Google Cloud Platform
- **Methodologies**: Clean Architecture, DDD, TDD
- **Patterns**: CQRS
- **Tools**: Flyway, SparkJava

## Work Environment
- Flexibility: Remote, in-office, or hybrid
- 120-day "workation" policy (work remotely abroad)
- Generous annual leave, paid birthday off
- Market-leading compensation
- Significant equity programme

## What They Look For
- Engineers who are technically skilled AND passionate about solving real-world problems
- People who thrive in dynamic environments
- Those who demonstrate ownership mentality
- Candidates who can "Deliver WOW"
- People who are passionate, adaptable, and ready to contribute to something bigger

## Recruiter Insights (from Apollinaria Sergeeva)
Apollinaria is a Java Backend Recruiter at Revolut. Key insights from her articles:

### What She Values:
1. **Clear career direction** - Know your career aspirations before interviewing
2. **Positive framing** - Focus on what you want to achieve, not what you're escaping
3. **Honesty about strengths** - Be transparent about what you know and don't know
4. **Openness** - Share both successes and failures
5. **Genuine engagement** - Treat recruiters as partners, not obstacles
6. **Good preparation** - Even if it's your area of expertise

### What She Looks For Beyond Hard Skills:
- How you work in a team
- How you communicate
- How proactive you are
- Ownership mentality
- Ready to "Deliver WOW"

### Her Philosophy:
- "Hard skills and experience are 50% of your success"
- Recruiters should be treated as career partners, not gatekeepers
- Values candidates who ask about culture and growth, not just benefits
- Appreciates candidates who are specific about what they're looking for
