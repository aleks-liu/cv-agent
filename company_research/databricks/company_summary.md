## Company Overview

- **Full Name:** Databricks, Inc.
- **Industry:** Data and AI / Enterprise Software / Cloud Computing
- **Founded:** 2013
- **Headquarters:** San Francisco, CA, USA (160 Spear Street, 15th Floor)
- **Size:** 7,000-8,000+ employees worldwide
- **Stage:** Late-stage private (valued at $134 billion, Series L)
- **Business Model:** B2B - Data Intelligence Platform (SaaS)

## What They Do

Databricks is the Data and AI company that provides a unified Data Intelligence Platform built on an open lakehouse architecture. The platform combines data lakes and data warehouses, enabling organizations to unify all their data and governance while leveraging AI models tuned to their unique characteristics. Databricks helps enterprises build and deploy secure data applications, AI apps, and intelligent agents.

## Mission & Vision

**Mission:**
> "To simplify and democratize data and AI, helping data and AI teams solve the world's toughest problems."

Databricks aims to make data intelligence accessible to everyone in an organization through automation and natural language, enabling both technical teams and non-technical users to discover and use data like experts.

**Vision:**
To be the leading Data Intelligence Platform that enables every organization to become a data + AI company. CEO Ali Ghodsi has expressed ambitions for Databricks to become a trillion-dollar company by dominating the transactional database market, AI agents, and Data Intelligent Applications.

## Core Values

Databricks operates on six core cultural principles that define how employees ("Bricksters") work, interact, and innovate:

1. **Customer Obsessed:** The customer is at the center of everything Databricks does. The company fully believes that what's best for their customers is best for the company.

2. **Bias for Action:** Bricksters value speed over inaction. They debate fast, plan fast, drive alignment and execute. Speed matters in everything they do.

3. **Raising the Bar:** Every new hire is an opportunity to make teams and the company better. Hiring principles don't compromise as the company scales - they maintain high standards regardless of growth.

4. **Truth-Seeking:** Databricks values radical candor and transparency. They seek the truth through honest, direct communication and data-driven decision making.

5. **First Principles:** Approaching problems from fundamental truths rather than assumptions or conventions, enabling innovative solutions.

6. **Company First:** In any work, Bricksters do what is right for Databricks and put the best interest of the company ahead of all other competing needs, while ensuring success for customers and colleagues.

## Technology Stack

**Core Platform Components:**
- **Apache Spark:** Distributed computing framework (created by Databricks founders)
- **Delta Lake:** Open-source storage layer for data lakes
- **MLflow:** Open-source ML lifecycle management (30M+ monthly downloads)
- **Unity Catalog:** Universal governance and security layer
- **Lakehouse Architecture:** Unified data warehouse + data lake

**Languages & Frameworks:**
- Python, SQL, Scala, R
- Spark, PySpark
- TensorFlow, PyTorch (for ML workloads)

**Infrastructure:**
- Multi-cloud: AWS, Azure, Google Cloud
- Serverless compute (including Serverless GPUs - A10g, H100)
- Apache Iceberg support

**Recent Product Innovations (2025):**
- **Lakebase:** Serverless Postgres database built for AI (powered by Neon acquisition)
- **Agent Bricks:** Automated AI agent building platform
- **Databricks Apps:** User experience layer for data and AI applications
- **Lakeflow:** Data engineering solution (ETL/ELT, orchestration)
- **MLflow 3.0:** Redesigned for GenAI with agent monitoring and tracing

**Confidence:** HIGH (from official sources, press releases, product documentation)
