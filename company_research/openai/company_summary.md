## Company Overview

- **Full Name:** OpenAI, Inc. (operating as OpenAI Group PBC since October 2025)
- **Industry:** Artificial Intelligence / Technology
- **Founded:** December 11, 2015
- **Headquarters:** San Francisco, California, USA
- **Size:** 3,000+ employees (grew from ~1,000 to 3,000+ in 2024-2025)
- **Stage:** Late-stage private (valued at $500B as of October 2025, potentially $830B in Q1 2026)
- **Business Model:** B2B and B2C - API platform, ChatGPT subscriptions, enterprise solutions

## What They Do

OpenAI is an AI research and deployment company building artificial general intelligence (AGI) that benefits all of humanity. They develop large language models (GPT series), reasoning models (o-series), and multimodal AI including video generation (Sora). Their flagship product ChatGPT has over 800 million weekly active users and serves 1 million business customers.

## Mission & Vision

**Mission:**
> "OpenAI's mission is to ensure that artificial general intelligence (AGI)---by which we mean highly autonomous systems that outperform humans at most economically valuable work---benefits all of humanity."

From the Charter: "We will attempt to directly build safe and beneficial AGI, but will also consider our mission fulfilled if our work aids others to achieve this outcome."

**Vision:**
To be on the cutting edge of AI capabilities while ensuring safety and broad benefit distribution. They aim to develop AGI responsibly with humanity's best interests as their primary fiduciary duty.

## Core Values

From OpenAI's official values:

1. **Humanity First:** Working at OpenAI means being part of a team passionate about benefitting people and society. "We build AI to elevate humanity."

2. **Act with Humility:** Recognizing the limits of knowledge and remaining open to new ideas, perspectives, and the possibility of being wrong. This influences their iterative deployment approach.

3. **Feel the AGI:** AGI will be powerful in an unprecedented way. Building it requires rigor, discipline, boundless imagination, and deep responsibility. They approach development with respect for the transformative technology they are creating.

4. **Ship Joy:** Through research they develop products that transform lives. Their technology reflects internal optimism and stewardship of the mission.

## Operating Principles

1. **Find a Way:** Belief in finding approaches that work. Agency given to individuals and teams. Ideas come from anywhere, regardless of title or tenure.

2. **Creativity Over Control:** Finding creative solutions, even if imperfect, over rigidity. Problem-solving based on first principles and best practices.

3. **Update Quickly:** Coming with hypotheses and willingness to change approach with new information. Seeking truth and adapting.

4. **Intense Focus:** Hard workers making world impact. Intensity and resilience pivotal to mission. Clarity enables hard decisions.

## Culture & Work Environment

- **Work Style:** Hybrid (office + remote flexibility), primarily San Francisco-based with global offices
- **Culture Traits:** Mission-driven, meritocratic, high-intensity, cutting-edge research, fast-paced

### Positive Aspects (from employee reviews):
- Talented, dedicated, mission-driven colleagues
- Very meritocratic - leaders promoted based on good ideas and execution
- Strong bias to action ("you can just do things")
- The best ideas tend to win
- Competitive compensation and benefits
- Bleeding-edge research work

### Challenges:
- Rapid growth causing organizational strain
- Management learning curve - "every week there's another crisis"
- Varying experiences by team - no single OpenAI experience
- Culture of secrecy due to high scrutiny
- Intense pace can lead to burnout risk
- Work-life balance rated lower (3.2/5 on Glassdoor)

### Employee Ratings:

- Culture & Values: 3.7/5

## Technology Stack

- **Primary Languages:** Python (main product code), C/C++ (performance-critical), Swift/SwiftUI (native apps)
- **Frameworks:** FastAPI (Python APIs), Temporal (async workflows)
- **Infrastructure:** Microsoft Azure (cloud partner), custom AI chips (with Broadcom, TSMC 3nm, 2026), Google TPUs (since June 2025)
- **ML/AI:** Reinforcement Learning from Human Feedback (RLHF), large-scale distributed training
- **Desktop/Browser:** OWL (OpenAI Web Layer) on Chromium, SwiftUI/AppKit for Atlas browser

**Confidence:** MEDIUM-HIGH (from engineering blog, job postings, public sources)

## Products & Services

### Current Model Lineup (as of late 2025/early 2026):
- **GPT-5 / GPT-5.2:** Latest flagship models, default in ChatGPT
- **GPT-5.2 Pro:** First model to cross 90% on ARC-AGI-1, strongest for math/science
- **o3 / o4-mini:** Reasoning models for complex tasks
- **Sora 2 / Sora 2 Pro:** Advanced video generation with audio sync
- **Codex:** Agentic coding assistant (GPT-5.1-Codex-Max)

### Platforms:
- **ChatGPT:** Consumer and enterprise AI assistant (800M+ weekly users)
- **API Platform:** Developer access to all models
- **ChatGPT Enterprise/Business/Edu:** Business-focused offerings
- **Atlas:** AI-native browser

## Security Context

### Security Operations:
- 24/7/365 on-call security team rotation
- Automated alerts and manual investigation processes
- Bug Bounty Program with safe harbor and cash rewards

### Compliance & Certifications:
- SOC 2 Type 2 certified
- ISO 27001, 27017, 27018, 27701 certified
- GDPR and CCPA compliant
- Data Processing Addendum available

### Safety Governance:
- **Safety and Security Committee (SSC):** Independent Board oversight committee
- Chaired by Zico Kolter
- Members include Adam D'Angelo (Quora CEO), General Paul Nakasone (ret.), Nicole Seligman (former Sony EVP/GC)

### Trust & Safety:
- Dedicated Trust & Safety team led by Dave Willner
- Applied safety researchers focused on model safety
- Trust Portal available at trust.openai.com

### Data Privacy:
- Enterprise/Business/Edu/API data NOT used for training by default
- Clear separation of consumer and enterprise data policies

**Confidence:** HIGH (from official security pages, compliance certifications)
